About Learning Tree Academy

Coaching Classes for Academic Excellence, built around confidence, clarity, and consistent growth for every child.

RS

Rohini Singh

Founder & Lead Educator

Rohini Singh brings years of student-centered teaching experience with a strong emphasis on making basics strong before moving to advanced learning. Her classroom style blends discipline with warmth so children stay motivated and fearless in learning.

GD Goenka Kothari International

At Learning Tree Academy, we support students from Classes 1 to 8 through structured academic coaching, chapter-wise concept clarity, regular assessments, and practical revision systems. Every child receives focused guidance based on their learning pace, school curriculum, and confidence level.

Our institute in Sushant Golf City, Lucknow, has become a trusted local learning space for families who value strong fundamentals, measurable progress, and a positive classroom environment. We offer both online and offline modes to make quality education accessible and convenient.
